Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Insulation

Body insulation is comprised of urethane, Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), foam and recycled felt. Insulation is used as a sound deadener to reduce exterior road and powertrain noises from the interior of the vehicle. Baffles are located in the bottom of the A-, B- and C-pillars. Mastic insulators are also used as insulation. For information on the location of the mastic insulators, refer to Section 501-35 . Insulation is installed:

  • above and below the instrument panel.
  • at the cowl side panels.
  • over the front and rear floor pan areas.
  • over the luggage compartment floor.
