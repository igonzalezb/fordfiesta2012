SECTION 501-02: Front End Body Panels
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Hood Alignment

  1. NOTE: To adjust the hood, the hood latch must be removed.

    Remove the hood latch. For additional information, refer to Section 501-14 .

  1. NOTE: It is not possible to adjust the rear of the hood to the height of the fender.

    Loosen the 4 hood hinge-to-hood nuts and the 4 hood hinge-to-body bolts until the hood will slightly move.
  1. Adjust the hood to align the hood-to-fender gaps. For additional information on body margins, refer to Section 501-35 .
  1. Tighten the 4 hood hinge-to-body bolts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Tighten the 4 hood hinge-to-hood nuts.
    • Tighten to 11Nm (97 lb-in).

  1. NOTE: NOTE: The clearances and edge alignment must be correct before the hood latch is installed.

    Install the hood latch. For additional information, refer to Section 501-14 .
    • The hood latch must be installed so that the hood engages without stress and the clearances on the LH and RH sides are not changed.
