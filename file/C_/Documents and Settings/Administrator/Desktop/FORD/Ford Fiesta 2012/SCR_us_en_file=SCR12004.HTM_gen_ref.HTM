SECTION 501-02: Front End Body Panels
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Fender Splash Shield

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the wheel and tire. For additional information, refer to Section 204-04 .
  1. If equipped, remove the front mudflaps.
  1. Remove the 8 front fender splash shield retainers and the front fender splash shield.
  1. To install reverse the removal procedure.
