SECTION 501-02: Front End Body Panels
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Fender

Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on the hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the headlamp assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 417-01 .
  1. Remove the front fender upper bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Gently pry out on the fixed front corner glass to gain access to the front fender upper rear bolt.
  1. Loosen the front fender upper rear bolt.
    • Calculate the correct torque wrench setting for the following torque. Refer to the Torque Wrench Adapter Formulas in the Appendix.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Remove the front fender splash shield. For additional information, refer to Fender Splash Shield in this section.
  1. Remove the front fender inner rear bolt and the 2 front fender lower bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Remove the 3 front bumper cover-to-front bumper cover bracket upper bolts and disconnect the front bumper cover from the front fender.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Remove the 3 front fender bracket bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Slide the front fender towards the front of the vehicle and remove.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
