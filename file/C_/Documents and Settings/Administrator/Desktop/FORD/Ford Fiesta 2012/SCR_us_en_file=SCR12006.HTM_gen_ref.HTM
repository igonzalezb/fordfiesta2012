Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front bumper cover. For additional information, refer to Section 501-19 .
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 scrivet retainers and both front lower side radiator air deflectors.
  1. Remove the 5 lower radiator air deflector bolts and the lower radiator air deflector.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Transfer parts as necessary.
