SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

A-Pillar Trim Panel

Removal and Installation

NOTICE: To avoid damage to the A-pillar trim panel, remove any retaining clips from the body and attach them to the A-pillar trim panel before installing.

  1. Position the door opening weatherstrip aside.
  1. Gently pull the upper A-pillar trim panel toward the center of the vehicle to release the A-pillar trim panel retaining clips.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
