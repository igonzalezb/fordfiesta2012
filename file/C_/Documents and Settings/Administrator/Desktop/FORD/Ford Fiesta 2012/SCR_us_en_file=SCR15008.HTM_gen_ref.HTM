SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Liftgate Trim Panel


Item Part Number Description
1 W712474 Upper liftgate trim panel retaining clip (4 required)
2 42906 (RH) / 42907 (LH) Upper liftgate trim panel
3 W700426 Upper liftgate trim panel screw (2 required)
4 W700426 Lower liftgate trim panel screw
5 46404 Lower liftgate trim panel
6 W712474 Lower liftgate trim panel retaining clip (10 required)

Upper liftgate trim panel

  1. Remove the upper liftgate trim panel retaining screw.
  1. Remove the upper liftgate trim panel.
    • Pull the upper liftgate trim panel toward the center of the vehicle and release the 2 upper liftgate trim panel retaining clips.

  1. NOTICE: To avoid damage to the upper liftgate trim panel, remove any retaining clips from the body and attach them to the upper liftgate trim panel before installing.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • To install, align the upper liftgate trim panel retaining clips to the retaining clip holes in the body

Lower liftgate trim panel

  1. Remove the lower liftgate trim panel.
    • Remove the lower liftgate trim panel retaining screw.
    • Pull the lower liftgate trim panel toward the front of the vehicle and release the 10 lower liftgate trim panel retaining clips.

  1. NOTICE: To avoid damage to the lower liftgate trim panel, remove any retaining clips from the body and attach them to the lower liftgate trim panel before installing.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • To install, align the lower liftgate trim panel retaining clips to the retaining clip holes in the body
