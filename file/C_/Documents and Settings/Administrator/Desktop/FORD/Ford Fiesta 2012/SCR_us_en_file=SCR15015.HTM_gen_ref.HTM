SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Headliner — 5-Door Hatchback

NOTE: Headliner with roof opening panel shown, all others similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 W711521 Trim pin (2 required)
2 51916 Headliner
3 W709571 Trim pin (2 required)
4 52124 Headliner retainer (if equipped)
5 W502660 Screw (6 required)
6 04104 (RH) / 04105 (LH) Sun visor assembly
7 04132 Sun visor clip (2 required)
8 519A70 Overhead console assembly (if equipped)

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the LH and RH sun visors.
    • Remove the 4 sun visor screws.
  1. Remove the LH and RH sun visor clips.
    • Remove the 2 sun visor clip screws.
  1. Remove the LH and RH A-pillar trim panels. For additional information, refer to A-Pillar Trim Panel in this section.
  1. Remove the LH and RH B-pillar upper and lower trim panels. For additional information, refer to B-Pillar Trim Panel in this section.
  1. Position aside the LH and RH C-pillar upper trim panels.

  1. NOTE: RH A-pillar shown, LH A-pillar similar.

    Disconnect the 2 electrical connectors located at the A-pillars.
    • Detach the 8 wire harness pin-type retainers.
    • Disconnect the rear glass washer hose at the LH A-pillar.
  1. If equipped, release the 2 wiring harness guides from the roof opening panel drain tubes.
  1. If equipped, remove the roof opening panel headliner retainer.
  1. Disconnect the rear view mirror electrical connector.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the roof opening panel motor electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the rear glass washer hose at the RH C-pillar.
  1. Lower the rear seat backs.

  1. NOTICE: An assistant is required when removing the headliner from the vehicle to avoid damage to the headliner.

    Remove the headliner.
    • Remove the 2 pushpins.
    • Remove the headliner through the rear hatch opening.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
