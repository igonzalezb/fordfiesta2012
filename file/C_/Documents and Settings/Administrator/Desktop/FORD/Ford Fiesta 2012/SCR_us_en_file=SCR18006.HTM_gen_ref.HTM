Spoiler — Roof

4 W713489 Roof-mounted spoiler clip (3 required)

7 13A613 High mounted stop lamp
8 46058 High mounted stop lamp screws (2 required)

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the high mounted stoplamp. For additional information, refer to Section 417-01 .
  1. Remove the roof mounted spoiler.
    1. Remove the roof mounted spoiler bolt covers.
    1. Remove the 2 roof mounted spoiler bolts.
      • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
    1. Remove the 4 roof mounted spoiler screws.
    1. Remove the 2 roof mounted spoiler screws behind the high mounted stoplamp.
    1. Disconnect the 3 roof mounted spoiler clips and remove the roof mounted spoiler.
      • Press down slightly on the front corner of the roof mounted spoiler to cause the rear corner to raise up slightly.
      • With your fingers behind the raised corner release the pressure on the front corner and pull gently out towards the rear of the vehicle to disconnect the clips and remove the spoiler.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
