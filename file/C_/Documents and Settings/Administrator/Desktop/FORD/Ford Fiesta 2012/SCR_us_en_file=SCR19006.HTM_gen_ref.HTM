SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Exterior Mirror

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the door speaker. For additional information, refer to Section 415-00 .
  1. Disconnect the electrical connector.
  1. Remove the exterior mirror nut cover.

  1. NOTE: When installing the mirror, make sure to align the locating pin to the locating hole.

    Remove the exterior mirror nut.
    • To install, tighten to 14 Nm (124 lb-in).
  1. Remove the exterior mirror.
    • Route the electrical wiring harness through the door.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
