Seat Armrest

Removal and Installation

NOTE: For parts identification and locations, refer to Seat â€” Exploded View, Front in this section.

  1. Pivot the armrest to the vertical position.
  1. Position aside the zipper cover and unzip the armrest cover.
  1. Partially invert the armrest cover to access the armrest bolt.
  1. Remove the armrest bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 4 Nm (35 lb-in).
  1. Remove the armrest.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
