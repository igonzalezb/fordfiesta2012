SECTION 501-10: Seating
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Seat Latch Striker — Rear

NOTE: RH shown, LH similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 62542 RH/62543LH Backrest striker
2 W701802 Backrest striker bolts (2 required without backrest retractor, 3 required with backrest retractor)

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the lower C-pillar trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Remove the bolts and the backrest striker.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
