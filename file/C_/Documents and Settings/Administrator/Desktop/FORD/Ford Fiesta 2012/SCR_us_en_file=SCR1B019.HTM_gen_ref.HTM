2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Window Regulator Motor — Rear Door

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Disconnect the window regulator motor electrical connector.
  1. Secure the window glass in position with tape.
  1. Remove the 3 window regulator motor screws.
  1. Remove the window regulator motor.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
