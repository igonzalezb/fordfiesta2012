2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Window Regulator — Rear Door

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door window glass. For additional information, refer to Window Glass — Rear Door in this section.
  1. Disconnect the speaker and window regulator motor electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 3 wire harness retainers and the door latch cable retainer.
  1. Remove the 3 window regulator motor bolts and the window regulator motor.
  1. Remove the 9 door carrier bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Slide the door carrier toward the front of the vehicle to release the door carrier retainers.
  1. Position aside the door carrier and disconnect the door lock actuator electrical connector.
  1. Route the interior door handle cable and door lock actuator electrical connector through the front door carrier.
  1. Remove the 2 window regulator bolts from the door carrier.
  1. Depress the retaining tabs and remove the window regulator from the door carrier.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
