SECTION 501-11: Glass, Frames and Mechanisms
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Door Glass Top Run — Rear

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door window glass. For additional information, refer to Window Glass — Rear Door in this section.
  1. Remove the 9 rear door carrier bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Slide the rear door carrier toward the front of the vehicle to release the door carrier retainers and position it aside.
  1. Remove the rear door glass top run.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
