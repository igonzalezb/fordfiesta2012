Removal and Installation

  1. On vehicles equipped with automatic transmission, remove the selector lever trim ring using a non-marring tool.
  1. Using a non-marring tool, lift the corners of the instrument panel lower finish panel upward to release the retaining clips.
    • Disconnect the electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the RH and LH floor console side trim panels.
    1. Remove the side panel push pins.
    1. Pull outward to release the floor console side panel clips.
  1. Apply the parking brake handle in the full upright position.
  1. Position the seats fully forward and remove the 2 floor console rear screws.
  1. Remove the 2 floor console front screws.
  1. Disconnect the 2 console electrical connectors on the right side and the 2 console electrical connectors on the left side. Separate the data cable pin type retainer from the console on the right side.
  1. Remove the floor console assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
