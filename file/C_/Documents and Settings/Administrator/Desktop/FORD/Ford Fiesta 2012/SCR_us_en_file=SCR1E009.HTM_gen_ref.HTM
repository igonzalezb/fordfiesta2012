2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Hood Latch

Removal and Installation

  1. NOTE: Mark the hood latch position prior to removal of the hood latch bolts.

    Remove the 2 hood latch bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Remove the hood latch.
    • Disconnect the hood latch from the hood latch cable.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • When the hood latch is installed, the latch adjustment must be checked to make sure the latch is installed and aligned correctly.
