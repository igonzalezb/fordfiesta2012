SECTION 501-14: Handles, Locks, Latches and Entry Systems
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Interior Door Handle — Front

NOTE: LH side shown, RH side similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 237W10 Interior door handle screw cover
2 46058 Interior door handle screw
3 22600 RH/ 22601 LH Interior door handle
4 221A00 Interior door handle cable

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the interior door handle screw cover.
  1. Remove the interior door handle screw.

  1. NOTICE: Do not pry outward at the bottom of the front interior door handle. Otherwise, damage to the front interior door handle may occur.

    Using a non-marring tool, pry outward at the top of the front interior door handle.
  1. Disconnect door latch cable from the front interior door handle.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the electrical connector.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
