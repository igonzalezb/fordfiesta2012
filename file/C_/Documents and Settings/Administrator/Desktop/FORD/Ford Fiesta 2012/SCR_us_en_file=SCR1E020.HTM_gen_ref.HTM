SECTION 501-14: Handles, Locks, Latches and Entry Systems
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Door Handle Reinforcement — Exterior, Rear

Removal and Installation

  1. NOTE: Do not separate the window regulator or window regulator motor from the front door carrier.

    Remove the window regulator. For additional information, refer to Section 501-11 .
  1. Remove the exterior rear door handle. For additional information, refer to Exterior Rear Door Handle in this section.
  1. Loosen, but do not remove, the exterior rear door handle reinforcement screw.
  1. Remove the 3 front door latch screws.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Remove the door latch, door carrier, and reinforcement assembly from vehicle.
    • Pull the front edge of the latch, carrier, and reinforcement assembly inward and then toward the front of the vehicle.
  1. Disconnect exterior rear door handle cable from the exterior rear door handle reinforcement.
  1. Separate the exterior rear door handle reinforcement from the exterior rear door handle reinforcement carrier.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
