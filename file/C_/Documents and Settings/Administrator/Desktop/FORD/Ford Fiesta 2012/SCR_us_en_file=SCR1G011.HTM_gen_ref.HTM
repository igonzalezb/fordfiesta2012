SECTION 501-16: Wipers and Washers
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Wiper/Washer Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 17A553 Steering column switch carrier
2 6475 Wiper/washer switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the steering column shrouds. For additional information, refer to the exploded view in Section 211-05 .
  1. Remove the wiper/washer switch.
    1. Disconnect the electrical connector.
    1. Release the wiper/washer switch retaining tab.
    1. Lift upward and remove the wiper/washer switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
