Removal and Installation

 WARNING: Carefully read cautionary information on product label. For EMERGENCY MEDICAL INFORMATION seek medical advice. In the USA or Canada on Ford/Motorcraft products call: 1-800-959-3673. For additional information, consult the product Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) if available. Failure to follow these instructions may result in serious personal injury.

NOTICE: Do not operate the washer pump prior to filling the windshield washer reservoir. Failure to follow these instructions may result in premature pump failure.

  1. Remove the LF headlamp assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 417-01 .
  1. Remove the upper washer reservoir bolt.
  1. Disconnect the washer reservoir hose(s).
  1. Remove the LH fender splash shield. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Remove the lower washer reservoir bolt and disconnect the electrical connector.
  1. To remove the washer reservoir, lift the washer reservoir slightly upward and then lower the washer reservoir.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
