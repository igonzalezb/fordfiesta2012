SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Air Deflector

Removal and Installation

  1. Place the roof opening panel in the full OPEN position.
  1. From the top of the vehicle and using a flat-blade screwdriver, press the spring steel locating tab down on each side while pushing the deflector toward the rear of the vehicle.
  1. Tilt the air deflector upward and pull forward to remove.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Make sure the locating tabs are locked firmly in place.
    • Initialize the roof opening panel motor. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.
