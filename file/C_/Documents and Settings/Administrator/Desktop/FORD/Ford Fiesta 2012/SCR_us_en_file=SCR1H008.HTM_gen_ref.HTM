SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Roof Opening Panel

Removal

  1. NOTE: Make sure the roof opening panel is in the CLOSED position.

    Remove the headliner. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Disconnect the 2 front and the 2 rear drain hoses from the roof opening panel.
  1. Disconnect the roof opening panel motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 10 roof opening panel frame bolts, the 2 roof opening panel frame bracket bolts and the roof opening panel.
    • With the aid of an assistant, compress the legs of the roof opening panel retaining clip and lower the roof opening panel.
  1. If installing a new roof opening panel frame, transfer components as necessary.

Installation

  1. NOTE: Make sure the roof opening panel is positioned correctly on the retaining clip before tightening the roof opening panel bolts.

    NOTE: Do not fully tighten the roof opening panel frame bolts at this stage.

    Compress the legs of the roof opening panel retaining clip and install the roof opening panel.
    • Loosely install the 10 roof opening panel frame bolts and the 2 roof opening panel frame bracket bolts.
  1. Tighten the 10 roof opening panel frame bolts and the 2 roof opening panel frame bracket bolts in the sequence shown.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Connect the roof opening panel motor electrical connector.
  1. Connect the 2 front and the 2 rear drain hoses to the roof opening panel.
  1. Install the headliner. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Initialize the roof opening panel motor. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.
