SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Roof Opening Panel Shield

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the roof opening panel glass. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Glass in this section.
  1. Slide the roof opening panel shield forward halfway.
  1. Disengage the roof opening panel shield guide feet.
    • Lift the front portion of the roof opening panel shield upward at center until able to disengage one of the front guide feet.
  1. With one of the guide feet released, carefully rotate the roof opening panel to disengage the remaining guide feet.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
