SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Roof Opening Panel Motor

Removal

NOTE: The roof opening panel motor must be initialized when repairs are carried out on any part of the roof opening panel system, including: whenever the roof opening panel motor has been removed from the roof opening panel system, the roof opening panel glass has been removed from the roof opening panel system, the roof opening panel assembly has been removed from the vehicle, a new roof opening panel motor has been installed, a new roof opening panel glass has been installed or a new roof opening panel assembly has been installed. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.

  1. Remove the LH and RH A- and B-pillar trim panels. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Remove the roof opening panel headliner retainer (trim ring).
  1. Remove the LH and RH sun visors.
  1. Position aside the front door weatherstrip as necessary and lower the front portion of the headliner.
  1. Disconnect the roof opening panel motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 3 bolts and the roof opening panel motor.

Installation

  1. Install the roof opening panel motor and the 3 bolts.
    • Tighten to 3 Nm (27 lb-in).
  1. Connect the roof opening panel motor electrical connector.
  1. Raise the front of the headliner and reposition the front door weatherstrip.
  1. Install the LH and RH sun visors.
  1. Install the roof opening panel headliner retainer (trim ring).
  1. Install the LH and RH A- and B-pillar trim panels. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Initialize the roof opening panel motor. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.
