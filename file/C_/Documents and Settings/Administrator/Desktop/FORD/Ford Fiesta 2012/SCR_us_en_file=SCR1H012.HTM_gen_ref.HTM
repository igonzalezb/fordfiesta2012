SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Trough Assembly

  1. Remove the roof opening panel glass. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Glass .
  1. Remove the roof opening panel, For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel .
  1. Using a suitable tool, pry the trough assembly guides from the trough assembly.
  1. Gently raise the trough assembly and slide the trough assembly guides under the trough assembly.
  1. Slide the trough assembly rearward until the trough assembly is approximately 76.2 mm (3 in) from the roof opening panels rear edge.

  1. NOTICE: Use care when disconnecting the trough assembly from the roof opening panel tracks to prevent damage to the trough assembly.

    Gently flex the roof opening panel tracks and release the trough assembly from the tracks.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
