SECTION 501-19: Bumpers
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Bumper Cover — Front

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the front wheels and tires. For additional information, refer to Section 204-04 .
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 front stone deflector extension retainers on both sides from inside the front wheel wells.
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 bolts and both of the front stone deflector extension.
  1. Remove both front fender splash shields. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Disconnect the front bumper cover from the bumper cover brackets.
    1. Remove the 3 front bumper cover-to-front bumper cover bracket bolts on both sides.
      • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
    1. Gently pull down on the front bumper cover to disconnect it from the bumper cover brackets on both sides.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the auxiliary parking lamp electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover bolt on the underside of vehicle.
  1. Remove the 4 upper radiator grille reinforcement-to-radiator support pin-type retainers and remove the front bumper cover assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Transfer all necessary components.
