SECTION 501-19: Bumpers
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Bumper — Front


Item Part Number Description
1 W709933 Front bumper bolt (8 required)
2 W701121 Front bumper-to-radiator grille opening panel reinforcement bolt (4 required)
3 W713533 Radiator grille opening panel reinforcement-to-front bumper bolts (2 required)
4 17B749/ 17B750 Front bumper mounting bracket (RH/LH)
5 17757 Front bumper
  1. Remove the front severity sensors. For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B .
  1. Disconnect the front severity sensors wiring harness retainers from the front bumper.
  1. Remove the both headlamp assemblies.
    1. Remove the 3 headlamp assembly bolts.
      • To install, tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
    1. Disconnect and remove both headlamp assemblies.
  1. Disconnect the horn electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the wiring harnesses attached to the front bumper.
    • 3 on the RH side.
    • 4 on the LH side.
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 radiator side air deflector upper retainers.
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 retainers and the both lower air deflectors.
  1. Disconnect and remove the ambient temperature sensor from the bottom of the front bumper.
  1. Support the underside of the radiator support housing prior to removing the front bumper bolts.
  1. Remove the 2 radiator grille opening panel reinforcement-to-front bumper bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 4 front bumper-to-radiator grille opening panel reinforcement bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Remove the radiator grille opening panel reinforcement support-front bumper bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. With the help of an assistant, remove the 8 front bumper bolts and remove the front bumper.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
