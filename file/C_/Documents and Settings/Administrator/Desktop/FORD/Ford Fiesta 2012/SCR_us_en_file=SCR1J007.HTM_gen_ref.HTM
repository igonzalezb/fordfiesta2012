SECTION 501-19: Bumpers
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Bumper — Rear

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear bumper cover. For additional information, refer to Bumper Cover — Rear in this section.
  1. Remove the rear bumper nuts.
    • 4-door sedan has 6 nuts (3 each side).
    • 5-door hatchback has 8 nuts (4 each side).
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the rear bumper.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
