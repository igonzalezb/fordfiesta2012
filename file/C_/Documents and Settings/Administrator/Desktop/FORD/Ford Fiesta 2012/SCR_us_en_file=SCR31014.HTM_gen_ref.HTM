SECTION 303-01: Engine — 1.6L
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Timing Belt Cover


Item Part Number Description
1 W714914 Coolant pump pulley bolt (3 required)
2 8509 Coolant pump pulley
3 W500215 Upper and lower timing belt cover bolt (12 required)
4 W500303 Upper timing belt cover bolt
5 6019 Upper timing belt cover
6 6019 Lower timing belt cover
7 W702251 Timing belt cover backplate bolt (4 required)
8 6019 Timing belt cover backplate

Removal

All timing belt covers

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .

Upper timing belt cover

  1. Loosen the 4 coolant pump pulley bolts.
  1. Remove the accessory drive belt. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .
  1. Remove the engine mount. For additional information, refer to Engine Mount in this section.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and coolant pump pulley.
  1. Remove the 1 engine mount stud.
  1. Remove the 9 bolts and the upper timing belt cover.

Lower timing belt cover

  1. Remove the crankshaft pulley. For additional information, refer to Crankshaft Pulley in this section.
  1. Remove the 3 bolts and the lower timing belt cover.

Timing belt cover backplate

  1. Loosen the 4 coolant pump pulley bolts.
  1. Remove the accessory drive belt. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .
  1. Remove the engine mount. For additional information, refer to Engine Mount in this section.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and coolant pump pulley.
  1. Remove the 1 engine mount stud.
  1. Remove the 9 bolts and the upper timing belt cover.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and the timing belt cover backplate.

Installation

Timing belt cover backplate

  1. Install the timing belt cover backplate and the 4 bolts.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Install the upper timing belt cover and the 9 bolts.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Install the 1 engine mount stud finger tight.

  1. NOTICE: The 2 engine mount studs must be torqued or damage to the powertrain may occur.

    Torque the 2 engine mount studs before installing the engine mount nuts.
    • Tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Install the coolant pump pulley and the 4 bolts.
    • Do not torque at this time.
  1. Install the engine mount. For additional information, refer to Engine Mount in this section.
  1. Install the accessory drive belt. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .
  1. Tighten the 4 coolant pump pulley bolts to 20 Nm (177 lb-in).

Lower timing belt cover

  1. Install the lower timing belt cover and the 3 bolts.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Install the crankshaft pulley. For additional information, refer to Crankshaft Pulley in this section.

Upper timing belt cover

  1. Install the upper timing belt cover and the 9 bolts.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Install the 1 engine mount stud finger tight.

  1. NOTICE: The 2 engine mount studs must be torqued or damage to the powertrain may occur.

    Torque the 2 engine mount studs before installing the engine mount nuts.
    • Tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Install the coolant pump pulley and the 4 bolts.
    • Do not torque at this time.
  1. Install the engine mount. For additional information, refer to Engine Mount in this section.
  1. Install the accessory drive belt. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .
  1. Tighten the 4 coolant pump pulley bolts to 20 Nm (177 lb-in).
