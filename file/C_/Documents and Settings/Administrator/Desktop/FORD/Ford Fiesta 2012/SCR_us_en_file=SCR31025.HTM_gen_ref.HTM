Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the engine oil filter gasket with clean engine oil prior to installing the oil filter.

    Remove the engine oil filter and discard.
    • To install, tighten to 15 Nm (133 lb-in).
  1. Using hose clamp pliers, clamp the 2 oil cooler coolant hoses and remove the 2 coolant hoses from the oil cooler.

  1. NOTICE: If metal or aluminum material is present in the oil cooler, mechanical concerns exist. Failure to correct these concerns may cause engine failure. To diagnose mechanical concerns, refer to Section 303-00 .

    Remove the oil cooler mounting bolt and the oil cooler.
    • Inspect the oil cooler gasket, replace if necessary.
    • To install, tighten to 56 Nm (41 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Inspect and adjust the coolant level in the degas bottle.
