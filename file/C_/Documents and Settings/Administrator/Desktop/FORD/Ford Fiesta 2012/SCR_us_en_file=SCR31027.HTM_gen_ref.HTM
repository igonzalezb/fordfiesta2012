Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the screw and the oil level indicator and tube.
    • Inspect and replace the O-ring seal, if necessary.
  1. Drain the engine oil.
    • Install the drain plug.
    • Tighten to 28 Nm (21 lb-ft).
  1. Remove and discard the engine oil filter.
  1. Remove the 17 bolts from the oil pan.
  1. Using the 3 pry pads (2 shown), remove the oil pan.

Installation

  1. NOTICE: Do not use metal scrapers, wire brushes, power abrasive discs or other abrasive means to clean the sealing surfaces. These tools cause scratches and gouges, which make leak paths. Use a plastic scraping tool to remove traces of sealant.

    Clean and inspect all mating surfaces using silicone gasket remover.
  1. Install 2 M8x20 studs for oil pan alignment.

  1. NOTE: The oil pan must be installed within 5 minutes of applying the sealant.

    NOTE: If the oil pan is not secured within 5 minutes of sealant application, the sealant must be removed and the sealing area cleaned with metal surface prep. Allow to dry until there is no sign of wetness, or 5 minutes, whichever is longer. Failure to follow this procedure can cause future oil leakage

    Apply a 3.5 mm (0.137 in) bead of sealant to the oil pan.
  1. Install the oil pan using the 2 studs for alignment.
  1. Install 15 bolts finger tight and remove the 2 alignment studs and install the remaining 2 bolts finger tight.
  1. Tighten the oil pan bolts in the following sequence in 3 stages.
    • Stage 1: Tighten bolts 1 through 4 to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
    • Stage 2: Tighten bolts 5 through 17 to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
    • Stage 3: Tighten bolts 5 through 17 to 20 Nm (177 lb-in).

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the engine oil filter gasket with clean engine oil prior to installing the oil filter.

    Install a new engine oil filter.
    • Tighten to 15 Nm (133 lb-in).

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the O-ring seal with clean engine oil.

    Install the oil level indicator and tube and the screw.
    • Tighten to 4 Nm (35 lb-in).
  1. Fill the engine with clean engine oil.
