SECTION 303-01: Engine — 1.6L
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Oil Pump Screen and Pickup Tube

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the engine oil pan. For additional information, refer to Oil Pan in this section.
  1. Remove the 3 bolts and the oil pump screen and pickup tube.
    • Inspect and replace the O-ring seal, if necessary.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
