SECTION 303-01: Engine — 1.6L
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Engine Mount


Item Part Number Description
1 W520214 Engine mount nut (2 required)
2 W706408 Engine mount bolt (3 required)
3 6038 Engine mount

Removal

  1. Release the 2 tabs and position the degas bottle aside.
  1. Using a floor jack and a wooden block, support the engine.

  1. NOTICE: The engine mount studs must be held while removing the engine mount nuts or damage to the powertrain may occur.

    NOTE: Do not loosen the engine mount center bolt or the engine may become improperly positioned.

    Use the holding feature to prevent the engine mount studs from turning, remove the 2 engine mount nuts.
  1. Remove the 3 bolts and the engine mount.

Installation

  1. NOTICE: The engine mount studs must be torqued or damage to the powertrain may occur.

    Torque the engine mount studs before installing the engine mount.
    • Tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).

  1. NOTE: Do not loosen the engine mount center bolt or the engine may become improperly positioned.

    Install the engine mount and the 2 nuts.
    • Tighten the 2 nuts to 80 Nm (59 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 3 engine mount bolts.
    • Tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Position the degas bottle on the 2 retainer tabs.
