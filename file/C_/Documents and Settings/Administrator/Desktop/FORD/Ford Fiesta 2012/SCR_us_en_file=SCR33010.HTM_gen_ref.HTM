SECTION 303-03: Engine Cooling
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Cooling Fan Motor and Shroud

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the air cleaner. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Detach the transmission vent hose from the cooling fan and shroud.
  1. Detach the upper 2 wiring harness pushpins from the cooling fan shroud.
  1. Release the 2 cooling fan shroud clips.
  1. Detach the lower 2 wiring harness pushpins from the cooling fan shroud and disconnect the cooling fan electrical connector.
  1. Remove the cooling fan and shroud.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
