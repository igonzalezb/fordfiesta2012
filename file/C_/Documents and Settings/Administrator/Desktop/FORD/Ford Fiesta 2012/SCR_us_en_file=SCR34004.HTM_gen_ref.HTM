SECTION 303-04: Fuel Charging and Controls — 1.6L
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Throttle Body


Item Part Number Description
1 — Electronic throttle control electrical connector (part of 12A581)
2 W708972 Throttle Body (TB) bolt (4 required)
3 9E926 TB
4 9E936 TB gasket

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the Air Cleaner (ACL) outlet pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Disconnect the electronic throttle control electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and the Throttle Body (TB).
    • Remove and discard the TB gasket.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Install a new TB gasket.
  1. If the TB was replaced, turn the key ON without cranking the engine for 1 minute to allow the PCM to learn the adaptive table values for the new TB .
