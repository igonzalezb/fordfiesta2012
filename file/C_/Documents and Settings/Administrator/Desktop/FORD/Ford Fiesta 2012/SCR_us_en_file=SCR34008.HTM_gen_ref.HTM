SECTION 303-04: Fuel Charging and Controls — 1.6L
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Fuel Pump Control Module


Item Part Number Description
1 — Fuel Pump (FP) control module electrical connector (part of 14A005)
2 9D370 FP control module
3 W711330 FP control module nut (2 required)

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the LH front seat. For additional information, refer to Section 501-10 .
  1. Remove the scuff plate trim panel and the cowl side trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .

  1. NOTE: The Fuel Pump (FP) control module is located on the rear surface of the LH front seat mount.

    Disconnect the FP control module electrical connector.

  1. NOTICE: Do not overtighten the fasteners or damage to the module may occur.

    Remove the 2 FP control module nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
