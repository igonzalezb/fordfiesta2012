Removal and Installation

  1. NOTICE: Spark plug wires must be connected correctly. For additional information, refer to the Coil Terminal-to-Cylinder Relationship illustration in the Description and Operation, Engine Ignition portion of this section. Failure to follow this instruction may result in poor engine performance.

    NOTICE: Do not pull on the spark plug wire as it may separate from the spark plug wire connector inside the spark plug wire boot.

    Disconnect the spark plug wires from the ignition coil by slightly twisting while pulling upwards.
  1. Disconnect the ignition coil electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 4 ignition coil bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Remove the ignition coil.

  1. NOTE: Wipe the coil towers with a clean cloth dampened with soap and water. Remove any soap film and dry with compressed air. Inspect for cracks, carbon tracking and dirt.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Apply silicone dielectric compound to the inside of the spark plug wire boots prior to installation.
