GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Toe Adjustment — Front

  1. Using a suitable steering wheel holding device, lock the steering wheel in the straight ahead position.
  1. Using alignment equipment and the manufacturer's instructions, measure the toe.
  1. Loosen the tie-rod end jam nuts.
  1. Rotate the tie rods as necessary to adjust the toe setting.
  1. Tighten the tie-rod end jam nuts to 85 Nm (63 lb-ft).
  1. Recheck the toe settings, adjust as necessary.
