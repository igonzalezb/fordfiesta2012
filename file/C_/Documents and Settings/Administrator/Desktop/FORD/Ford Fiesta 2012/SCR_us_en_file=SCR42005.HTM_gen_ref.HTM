SECTION 204-02: Rear Suspension
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Wheel Bearing and Wheel Hub


Item Part Number Description
1 W711918 Wheel bearing and wheel hub assembly bolt (4 required) (install new)
2 1109 Wheel bearing and wheel hub assembly

Removal

NOTICE: Suspension fasteners are critical parts that affect performance of vital components and systems. Failure of these fasteners may result in major service expense. Use the same or equivalent parts if replacement is necessary. Do not use a replacement part of lesser quality or substitute design. Tighten fasteners as specified.

  1. Remove the brake drum. For additional information, refer to Section 206-02 .
  1. Remove the wheel speed sensor bolt and remove the wheel speed sensor.
  1. Remove the wheel bearing and wheel hub assembly.
    • Remove and discard the 4 wheel bearing and wheel hub assembly bolts.
    • Remove the wheel bearing and wheel hub assembly.

Installation

  1. Install the wheel bearing and wheel hub assembly with 4 new bolts in the sequence shown.
    1. Tighten all 4 bolts to 65 Nm (48 lb-ft).
    1. Loosen each bolt 90 degrees.
    1. Tighten each bolt to 65 Nm (48 lb-ft) using the sequence shown.
  1. Install the wheel speed sensor and install the wheel speed sensor bolt.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Install the brake drum. For additional information, refer to Section 206-02 .
