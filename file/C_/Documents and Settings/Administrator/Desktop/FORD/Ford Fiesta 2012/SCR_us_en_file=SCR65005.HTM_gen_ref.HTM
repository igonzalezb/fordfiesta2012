Removal and Installation

  1. Refer to the procedures and/or exploded views in this section for any Warnings, Notices, Notes, Materials, Specifications and Special tools. Items in the exploded views may not be listed in order of removal.
  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the driver seat and the floor console to access the parking brake control.
    • For the driver seat, refer to Section 501-10 .
    • For the floor console, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Loosen the front parking brake cable adjuster nut 5 turns to release the tension for parking brake service. Remove the floor console finish panel to access the nut. For additional information, refer to the floor console procedure exploded view in Section 501-12 .
  1. Remove the crossmember and lower the exhaust and the exhaust heat shields in order to access the parking brake equalizer.
  1. Adjust the parking brake cables after parking brake component service. For additional information, refer to Parking Brake Cable Adjustment in this section.
