2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Wheel Speed Sensor — Front

NOTE: LH shown, RH similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 2C204 Wheel speed sensor
2 W500011 Wheel speed sensor bolt

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the wheel and tire. For additional information, refer to Section 204-04 .
  1. Position the rear portion of the splash shield aside.
    1. Remove the 3 pushpin retainers.
    1. Position the splash shield aside.
  1. Disconnect the wheel speed sensor electrical connector.
  1. Detach the wheel speed sensor from the body and wheel knuckle.
  1. Remove the wheel speed sensor bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Remove the wheel speed sensor.

  1. NOTICE: The white stripe on the wheel speed sensor harness is used for orientation purposes. Correct routing of the wheel speed sensor and harness is essential to prevent contact damage to the harness.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
