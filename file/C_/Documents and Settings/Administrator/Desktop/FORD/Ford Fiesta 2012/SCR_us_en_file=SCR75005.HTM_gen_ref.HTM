SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Selector Lever Cable Adjustment

  1. Disconnect the selector lever cable end from the manual control lever.
  1. Place the manual control lever in the D position as indicated on the transaxle case.
  1. Place the manual control lever in the P position as indicated on the transaxle case.
  1. Place the manual control lever in the D position as indicated on the transaxle case.
  1. Place the selector lever in the D position.
  1. Unlock the selector lever cable adjuster by sliding the locking tab over.
  1. Slide the selector lever cable end forward or backward to align it with the manual control lever.
  1. With the selector lever cable adjuster locking tab released, connect the selector lever cable end to the manual control lever.
  1. Slide the locking tab back to lock the selector lever cable adjuster.
  1. Verify that the vehicle only starts in PARK and NEUTRAL and the reverse lamps illuminate in REVERSE.
