SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Selector Lever Knob

Removal

  1. Remove the selector lever trim ring.
  1. Remove the upper console panel.
  1. Slide the selector lever knob bezel downward.
  1. Remove and discard the 2 selector lever knob screws.
  1. Remove the center console LH panel.
  1. Disconnect the Transmission Control Switch (TCS) electrical connector and remove the wiring harness fastener from the selector lever housing.
  1. Remove the selector lever knob.

Installation

  1. NOTICE: Do not overtighten the selector lever knob screws or damage to the selector lever or selector lever knob may occur.

    Install the selector lever knob and the 2 new selector lever knob screws.
    • Tighten to 2 Nm (18 lb-in).
  1. Slide the selector lever knob bezel up and into place.
  1. Connect the TCS electrical connector and install the wiring harness fastener to the selector lever housing.
  1. Install the center console LH panel.
  1. Install the upper console panel.
  1. Install the selector lever trim ring.
  1. Verify the selector lever knob functions correctly.
