SECTION 307-11: Automatic Shifting Manual Transmission/Transaxle
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Clutch Motor — No. 2


Material
Item Specification
High Temperature Nickel Anti-Seize Lubricant
XL-2 		—

Removal

  1. Clean the area around the clutch motor to ensure no debris gets into the clutch motor pocket.
  1. Disconnect the clutch motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 4 clutch motor bolts and the clutch motor.

Installation

  1. Inspect the clutch motor pocket for damage or debris. Clean or repair as needed.
  1. Apply anti-seize to the clutch motor surfaces as shown.
  1. Apply anti-seize to the clutch actuator lever splines as shown.

  1. NOTE: Clutch motor splines may need to be turned to ease installation.

    Install the clutch motor and the 4 clutch motor bolts.
    • Tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
  1. Connect the clutch motor electrical connector.
