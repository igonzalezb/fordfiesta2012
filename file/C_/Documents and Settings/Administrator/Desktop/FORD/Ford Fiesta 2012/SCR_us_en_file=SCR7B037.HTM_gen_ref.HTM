SECTION 307-11: Automatic Shifting Manual Transmission/Transaxle
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Transmission Range (TR) Sensor


Special Tool(s)
Vehicle Communication Module (VCM) and Integrated Diagnostic System (IDS) software with appropriate hardware, or equivalent scan tool

Removal

  1. Disconnect the Transmission Range (TR) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 2 TR sensor bolts and the TR sensor.

Installation

  1. Install the TR sensor and the 2 TR sensor bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Connect the TR sensor electrical connector.
  1. Adjust the selector lever cable. For additional information, refer to Section 307-05 .
  1. Re-calibrate the TR sensor. For additional information, refer to Transmission Adaptive Learning in this section.
  1. Verify the vehicle starts in PARK and NEUTRAL only.
