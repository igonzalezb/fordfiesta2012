SECTION 308-06: Manual Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Gearshift Lever


Item Part Number Description
1 7213 Gearshift lever
3 W706131 Gearshift lever nuts (4 required)
3 — Gearshift lever base (part of 7210)
4 — Shift cable (part of 7E395)
5 — Selector cable (part of 7E395)

Removal

  1. Remove the floor console. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Depower the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS). For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B .
  1. Remove the gearshift cables.
  1. Using a suitable tool, remove the wiring harness from the gearshift lever base.
  1. Disconnect the RCM electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 4 gearshift lever nuts and remove the gearshift lever.

Installation

  1. Install the gearshift lever and the 4 gearshift lever nuts.
    • Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Connect the RCM electrical connector.
  1. Install the wiring harness to the gearshift lever base.
  1. Install the gearshift cables.
  1. Install the floor console. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Repower the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS). For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B .
  1. Adjust the gearshift mechanism. For additional information, refer to Section 308-00 .
