Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the upper instrument panel finish panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Position aside the Front Controls Interface Module (FCIM) finish panel in the following order.
    1. Remove the 4 upper screws.
    1. Pull outward to detach the 2 lower clips.
    1. Disconnect the FCIM electrical connector and position the FCIM finish panel aside.
  1. Release the 2 start/stop switch tabs (upper and lower) and detach the start/stop switch from the instrument panel.
  1. Disconnect the start/stop switch electrical connector and remove the start/stop switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
