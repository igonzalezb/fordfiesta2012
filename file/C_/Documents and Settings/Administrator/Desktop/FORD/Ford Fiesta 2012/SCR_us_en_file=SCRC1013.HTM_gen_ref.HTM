SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Air Inlet Duct


Item Part Number Description
1 W506945 Air inlet duct screw (4 required)
2 19A618 Air inlet duct

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the heater core and evaporator core housing. For additional information, refer to Heater Core And Evaporator Core Housing in this section.
  1. Remove the 4 air inlet duct screws.
  1. Remove the air inlet duct.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
