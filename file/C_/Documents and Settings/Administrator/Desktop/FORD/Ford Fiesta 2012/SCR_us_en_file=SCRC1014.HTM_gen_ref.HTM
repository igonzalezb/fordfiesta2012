SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Blower Motor

Removal and Installation

  1. With the ignition in the ON position , select the PANEL climate control mode. Switch the ignition to the OFF position.
  1. Remove the brake pedal and bracket assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 206-06 .
  1. Disconnect the blower motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 3 blower motor screws.
  1. Remove the blower motor.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
