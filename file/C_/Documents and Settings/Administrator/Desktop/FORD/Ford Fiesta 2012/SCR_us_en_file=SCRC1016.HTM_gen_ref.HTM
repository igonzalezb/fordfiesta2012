Removal

  1. Remove the A/C compressor. For additional information, refer to Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor in this section.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
    1. Using the Compressor Clutch Holding Tool, hold the A/C clutch disc and hub.
    1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub spacer(s).
  1. Remove the A/C compressor pulley snap ring.

  1. NOTICE: Do not use air tools. Damage to the Air Conditioning (A/C) compressor pulley or A/C compressor may result.

    Remove the A/C compressor pulley.
    1. Install the Compressor Pulley Remover.
    1. Remove the A/C compressor pulley.

  1. NOTICE: Do not use air tools. Damage to the Air Conditioning (A/C) clutch field coil or A/C compressor may result.

    Remove the A/C clutch field coil.
    1. Install the A/C Compressor Field Coil Remover.
    1. Install the Compressor Pulley Remover.
    1. Remove the A/C clutch field coil.

Installation

  1. Clean the A/C clutch field coil and pulley mounting surfaces.

  1. NOTICE: Do not use air tools. Damage to the Air Conditioning (A/C) clutch field coil or A/C compressor may result.

    Install the A/C clutch field coil.
    1. Place the A/C clutch field coil on the A/C compressor with the A/C clutch field coil electrical connector correctly positioned.
    1. Place the A/C Compressor Field Coil Installer on the A/C clutch field coil.
    1. Place the A/C Compressor Coil Installer on the A/C Compressor Field Coil Installer.
    1. Use the Compressor Pulley Remover to install the A/C clutch field coil.
  1. Install the A/C compressor pulley.
  1. Install the A/C compressor pulley snap ring.
  1. Install the A/C clutch disc and hub spacer(s) and the A/C clutch disc and hub.
  1. Install the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
    1. Hold the A/C clutch disc and hub with the Compressor Clutch Holding Tool.
    1. Tighten to 13 Nm (115 lb-in).
  1. Measure and adjust the clutch air gap by adding or removing A/C clutch disc and hub spacers. For additional information, refer to the A/C clutch air gap procedure in Section 412-00 .
  1. Install the A/C compressor. For additional information, refer to Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor in this section.
