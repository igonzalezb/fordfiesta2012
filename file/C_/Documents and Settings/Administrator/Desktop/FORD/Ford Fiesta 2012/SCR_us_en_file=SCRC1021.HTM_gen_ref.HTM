Evaporator to Compressor Suction Line

  1. Recover the refrigerant. For additional information, refer to Section 412-01 .
  1. Remove the evaporator outlet line fitting nut.
    • Discard the O-ring seal.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Remove the rubber cap from the A/C compressor suction fitting stud.
  1. Remove the A/C compressor suction fitting nut.
    • Discard the gasket seal.
    • To install, tighten to 15 Nm (133 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Install a new gasket seal and new O-ring seals.
    • Add the correct amount of clean PAG oil to the refrigerant system. For additional information, refer to Section 412-00 .
  1. Evacuate, leak test and charge the refrigerant system. For additional information, refer to Section 412-00 .
