SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/19/2011

Mode Door Actuator — Air Inlet Door

Removal and Installation

  1. Lower and remove the glove compartment.
  1. Disconnect the air inlet mode door actuator electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 2 air inlet mode door actuator screws.
  1. Remove the air inlet mode door actuator.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
