SECTION 415-00: Information and Entertainment Systems
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Audio Input Jack


Item Part Number Description
1 19C149 Console bezel
2 19A164 Audio input jack
3 14D202 Universal Serial Bus (USB) port and cable

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the instrument panel lower finish panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. With a non-marring tool, lift the corners of the console bezel upward to release the retaining clips.
  1. Disconnect the electrical connector from the audio input jack.
  1. Release the tabs and remove the audio input jack from the bezel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Make sure to align the finish panel retaining clips to the adjacent retaining clip holes.
