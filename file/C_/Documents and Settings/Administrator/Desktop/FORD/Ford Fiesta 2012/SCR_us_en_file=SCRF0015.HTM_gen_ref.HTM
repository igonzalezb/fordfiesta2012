Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the upper finish panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Remove the 4 Front Controls Interface Module (FCIM) finish panel screws and rotate upwards from the bottom to release the 3 clips. Remove the FCIM finish panel.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector and release the Passenger Air Bag Deactivation (PAD) indicator from the FCIM finish panel.
  1. Remove the 4 screws from the FCIM and remove the FCIM from the finish panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
