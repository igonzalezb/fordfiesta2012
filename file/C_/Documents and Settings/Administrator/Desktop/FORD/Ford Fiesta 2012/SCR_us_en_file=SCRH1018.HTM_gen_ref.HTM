Removal and Installation

4-Door Sedan

  1. Remove the high mounted stoplamp assembly cover.
  1. Disconnect the 2 clips on the side of the high mounted stoplamp assembly and lower the high mounted stoplamp assembly down.
  1. Disconnect the high mounted stoplamp assembly electrical connector and remove.

5-Door Hatchback

  1. Remove the high mounted stoplamp assembly.
    1. Remove the 2 high mounted stoplamp assembly screws.
    1. Disconnect the high mounted stoplamp assembly electrical connector and the rear window washer hose and remove the high mounted stoplamp assembly.

All Vehicles

  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
