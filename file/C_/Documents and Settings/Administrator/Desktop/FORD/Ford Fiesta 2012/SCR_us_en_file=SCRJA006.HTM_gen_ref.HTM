SECTION 419-10: Multifunction Electronic Modules
2012 Fiesta Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/15/2011

Body Control Module (BCM)


Special Tool(s)
Vehicle Communication Module (VCM) and Integrated Diagnostic System (IDS) software with appropriate hardware, or equivalent scan tool


Item Part Number Description
1 — Electrical connectors (part of 14A005)
2 — Electrical connectors (part of 14K024)
3 W520412-S2 Body Control Module (BCM) nut (2 required)
4 15K600 BCM

Removal

NOTICE: Electronic modules are sensitive to static electrical charges. If exposed to these charges, damage to the module may result.

NOTE: Prior to the replacement of the module, it is necessary to upload the module configuration information to a scan tool. This information must be downloaded into the new Body Control Module (BCM) after installation. For additional information, refer to Section 418-01 . A minimum of 2 devices are necessary to complete the configuration.

  1. NOTE: This step is only necessary if the BCM is being replaced.

    Upload the module configuration information from the BCM into the scan tool. For additional information, refer to Section 418-01 .
  1. Remove the RH dash closeout panel.
  1. Disconnect the 7 electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 2 nuts and the BCM .

Installation

  1. Position the BCM and install the 2 nuts.
    • Tighten to 13 Nm (115 lb-in).

  1. NOTE: If the BCM is not being replaced, this is the last step that is necessary.

    Connect the 7 electrical connectors.
  1. Install the RH dash closeout panel.
  1. Download the BCM configuration information from the scan tool to the BCM . For additional information, refer to Section 418-01 .
  1. Carry out the BCM self-test to confirm all DTCs have been cleared.
  1. For vehicles without IA , program all the RKE transmitters into the new BCM . For additional information, refer to Section 501-14 .
